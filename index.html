Sleep is important!

You need at least 7-9 hours of it!

Children need more sleep than adults. The amount of sleep you need depends on your age.
More info
  • Infants need about 14-17 hours of sleep per day.
  • Teenagers need about 8-10 hours of sleep per day.
  • Adults need about 7-9 hours of sleep per day.
Learn more

If you don't sleep enough

(Chronic poor sleep)

Then you could get dementia, heart disease, type 2 diabetes, obesity and certain cancers.

Learn more

How do good sleep?

Three tips on getting good sleep

  1. Don't take naps in the afternoon.
  2. Avoid screens and electronic devices near bedtime.
  3. Avoid Caffeine.
    Caffeine can take 6-8 hours to wear off completely.
